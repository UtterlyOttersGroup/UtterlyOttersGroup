Once upon a time, there was an otter..

The otter wanted to build his own kingdom. To be a brave king, he went on an adventure. He defeated fishes, shrimps, clams… and sharks, granted himself a nice shark costume. Getting bit by a zombie, he never gave up. He succeeded in claiming a shark tooth even after death. The otter even acquired a mystery magic of fire and ice. Finally, the Ottorious Otter King, “Notor” has come! Ages have passed since “Notor” built the Otter Kingdom, and its name has changed to the Utterly Otters Group, aka UOG. UOG remained the best specie among all the creatures. Not only they are brave, but wise and smart. Now UOG is the biggest group on the planet Otty where magic and science coexist!
